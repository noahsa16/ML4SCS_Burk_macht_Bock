06 /

about

ML4SCS · Leuphana
research question

Can general handwriting activity be reliably detected from Apple Watch IMU data alone — binary writing-vs-idle classification on the wrist-worn accelerometer + gyroscope stream, independent of who is wearing the watch and what is being written? The Moleskine Smart Pen (NWP-F130) provides ground-truth labels only during data collection; at inference time the watch is the sole input.

data pipeline

Apple Watch → WatchStreamer app → iPhone bridge → FastAPI server → watch CSV. Moleskine Pen → BLE (pen_logger.py) → pen CSV. Both streams keep their device timestamps; server receive times are capture metadata and a coarse session anchor.

team
