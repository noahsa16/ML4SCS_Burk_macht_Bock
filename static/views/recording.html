Recording

Dual-stream capture — Apple Watch IMU + Moleskine pen dots, both streamed live and labelled by hand on the session.

/ three steps to start
  1. 01
    Connect the pen
    Power on the Moleskine pen, then click the status panel in the top-right and hit connect.
  2. 02
    Start the Watch app
    Open WatchStreamer on your Apple Watch — it streams 50 Hz IMU samples to this dashboard.
  3. 03
    Press start
    When both pen and watch report green in the topbar status panel, hit START below.
